Connect your Dropbox to the Android-x86 Nightlies tool!

Hungry for content? Go straight to the index

Step 0: No Dropbox account? use my referral link to sign up for an account, and we will each get a 256MB bonus!

Step 1: log in with Dropbox: oauth workflow; here, you can authorize the Nightlies Dropbox Tool to access the files in your Dropbox. Only files in Public/android-x86/ will be catalogged by this application. You can still maintain personal files in the Dropbox root or in other folders. Once you have authorized this application, you will not need to use your login credentials again.

Step 2: organize your builds in Public/android-x86/; make it a public folder, and let the files that you want to share synchronize with Dropbox. Other site users will be able to view and rate your builds. The highest rated builds will be promoted onto the mirror site martyfunkhouser, where there is no rate limiting or bandwidth usage cap.

We have about 5GB of space for promoted builds and server overhead, which is free for our excellent example of non-profit use, thanks to the awesome Xen Cloud of Computer Science House at the Rochester Institute of Technology.

Step 3: promote the Nightlies Dropbox Tool to your Android-x86 friends so they can vote for your uploads and review your builds or android apps! You are welcomed to donate your cpu time with a nightly build cron job to provide nightlies for your favorite TARGET_PRODUCT. You can even use this site as a way to promote your own original content.

Good Ideas and Legaleze

You can post anything from full builds of Android-x86 that you made with repo and lunch, apk's for your own projects, or tarballs of your own app sources.

(Do obey copyright, unusual uploads without clear licensing will not be promoted.)

We cannot be held responsible for illegal content that you provide (illegal files will not be promoted), and users who abuse the service in this way will be terminated, banned from contributing to the index, and probably also banned from the Dropbox community for violation of terms of service.
